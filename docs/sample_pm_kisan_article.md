1. Title: Get ₹6,000 Every Year: A Simple Guide to PM-KISAN for Farmers

---

2. Summary:
Hi there! Are you a farmer in India? The government has a great plan to help you. It is called PM-KISAN. This scheme gives you ₹6,000 every year. It is not a loan. It is a gift to help you with your farm. You get the money in three parts. Each part is ₹2,000. It goes straight into your bank account. Let us see how you can get this help.

---

3. What is the Scheme?:
The PM-KISAN scheme is a government program. It helps landholding farmers across India.
- Purpose: To give farmers extra cash for seeds and fertilizers.
- Department: It is run by the Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare.

---

4. Key Benefits:
This scheme is very helpful for your daily farm needs.
- Money: You get ₹6,000 every single year.
- Payments: You get ₹2,000 every four months.
- Direct Pay: The money goes right into your bank account. No middleman.
- Free: You do not have to pay anything back. It is 100% free from the government.

---

5. Eligibility Criteria:
Checking if you can join is easy:
- You must live in India.
- You must own farming land in your name.
- Your name must be in the local land records.
- Both small and big farmers can apply now.

---

6. Who Should Apply:
- Any farmer who owns a bit of land for crops.
- Families who need a little help to buy farm tools.
- People with a bank account and an Aadhaar card.

---

7. Who Should NOT Apply:
Some people cannot join this plan:
- People who work in big government offices.
- People who pay income tax.
- Doctors, lawyers, or engineers.
- People who get more than ₹10,000 in pension.
- Former ministers or mayors.

---

8. Documents Required:
Please keep these ready:
- Your Aadhaar Card.
- Your land papers (like your passbook or registration).
- Your bank account details (IFSC code and account number).
- Your phone number linked to Aadhaar.

---

9. Selection / Approval Process:
How do you get approved?
- First, the local village officers check your land papers.
- They make sure your name matches your Aadhaar card.
- Then, they upload your name to the official list.
- Once the list is ready, the government starts sending the money.

---

10. How to Apply:
You can do this yourself or go to a local center:
- Step 1: Visit the official PM-KISAN website (link below).
- Step 2: Click on New Farmer Registration.
- Step 3: Type in your Aadhaar number and your state.
- Step 4: Fill in the form with your name and land details.
- Step 5: Upload a photo of your land papers.
- Step 6: Hit submit and save your ID number.
- Step 7: Make sure to finish your e-KYC on the website. This is very important!

---

11. Important Dates:
- You can apply any time of the year.
- Money usually comes in April, August, and December.

---

12. Official Website / Application:
Visit this link to start your application:
https://pmkisan.gov.in/

---

13. FAQs:
Q: How much total money will I get?
A: You will get ₹6,000 every year in your bank account.

Q: Do I need an Aadhaar card?
A: Yes. You cannot apply without an Aadhaar card.

Q: My father owns the land. Can I apply?
A: No. The land must be in your own name in the government papers.

Q: Who can I talk to if I have a problem?
A: You can call the helpline at 155261 for help.

Q: What is e-KYC?
A: It is a quick check to prove it is really you. You can do it on the portal.

---

14. Pro Tips / Insights:
- Check your spelling: Make sure your name is the same on your land papers and Aadhaar card.
- Mobile link: Link your phone number to your Aadhaar card. It makes things much faster.
- Visit your bank: Ask your bank to link your Aadhaar card to your account (DBT).
